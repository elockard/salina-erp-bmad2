Design Notes

  • Theme 1 (Editorial Navy): Most traditional, trustworthy feel. Navy conveys financial accuracy and professionalism. Closest to classic ERP aesthetic.
  • Theme 2 (Sage Green): Balanced and calm. Green suggests growth and productivity without being too bold. Good for author-friendly publishers.
  • Theme 3 (Slate Gray): Closest to SAP Business One's enterprise precision. Neutral, data-focused, perfect for power users who prioritize function over style.
  • Theme 4 (Deep Purple): Most distinctive and creative. Purple suggests creativity and literary heritage. Best for publishers with strong brand identity.
